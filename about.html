About Poshan Care

Learn about our mission, our journey, and the team behind our fight against childhood malnutrition.

Our Story

Poshan Care was founded in 2025 with a simple yet powerful mission: to fight childhood malnutrition and ensure every child has access to nutritious meals. Our journey began with a small team of passionate individuals who believed that no child should go hungry.

Today, we work tirelessly to provide life-saving nutrition, promote healthy eating habits, and empower communities to break the cycle of malnutrition. We are driven by the belief that every child deserves a chance to thrive. Through partnerships, donations, and community programs, we have impacted thousands of lives and continue to expand our reach.

Our Journey

2025

The Beginning

Poshan Care was founded by a group of nutritionists and social workers concerned about the rising rates of childhood malnutrition.

2026

First Community Program

Launched our first nutrition program in a rural community, providing meals to 100 children daily.

2027

Expanding Our Reach

Expanded to 5 more communities and established partnerships with local schools and healthcare facilities.

2028

Education Initiative

Launched our nutrition education program for parents and caregivers, teaching sustainable healthy eating practices.

Present

Growing Impact

Now serving over 5,000 children across 25 communities with nutritious meals and education programs.
