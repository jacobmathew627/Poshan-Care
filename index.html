Our Mission

At Poshan Care, we are dedicated to eradicating childhood malnutrition by providing essential nutrients, promoting healthy eating habits, and supporting communities in need.

We believe that every child deserves access to nutritious food for proper growth and development. Through our programs, we aim to break the cycle of malnutrition and create lasting change in vulnerable communities.

About Our Work
